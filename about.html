About expert networks

Conferences are great, but a lot of science happens in the time between annual events taking place. And when they do happen, many of the best exchanges occur on the fringes. Expert Networks was borne out of a desire to allow the most engaged stakeholders in key scientific communities to continue to engage in a value-adding way all year round.

What we do

Niche
products

We create niche products for people who need access to timely information to avoid common road blocks and to move forward faster.

Life
sciences

Our focus is the high growth life sciences industry where the right insight makes all the difference.

Ready-made networks

By creating ready-made networks of hand-picked peers working in a facilitated, structured but boundaryless way throughout the year

Accelerate performance

We accelerate the performance of businesses and the delivery of better drugs to patients faster all around the world.

How we do it

Clarity

Hanson Wade gives life sciences leaders the clarity to see where opportunity lies and helping companies move forward faster is at the heart of everything we do.

Co-creation

We work with the most engaged participants of conferences in highly specialised scientific areas to co-create networks which serve as extensions of those events.

Custodians

Beyond being the catalyst to get them started, we are the custodians of the communities on behalf of their members, engaged in the constant pursuit of added value.

Communication

By focusing on emerging therapeutic areas where quality of information is the difference between success and failure, we help get better drugs to patients faster.
