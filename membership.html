Membership

Members of Expert Networks agree that if you want to go fast, you go alone; but if we want to go far, we go together. We bring like-minded companies together to engage in essential, collaborative, pre-competitive conversations to help them overcome the shared barriers to drug development success.

Find Your Expert Network

How it works

Invite only

The most invested companies in a specific scientific area are invited to join, meeting virtually for 90 mins, between 4 and 6 times per year.

Champions

Discussions are led by one internal champion from each member company who has strategic oversight over its work in the scientific area.

Curated

To ensure maximum value, members are curated based on development stage (with those either in or close to the clinic having priority).

Problem solving

As experience sharing networks evolve, problem-solving workstreams are developed offering members the chance to involve colleagues.

Motivated

We work to ensure that the only members that join are those motivated by what can be shared rather than those defined by what can’t.

Diverse

Network membership increases each year to ensure participants continue to benefit from a broad diversity of perspectives from peers.

What it includes

Vital
dialogue

Facilitated, frequent, virtual meetings help like-minded leaders to solve shared scientific problems.

Essential
resources

Pre-meeting resources, post-meeting summaries, and member-generated content are all shared.

Annual
event

Members influence the agenda and get priority access to the annual community in-person event.

Regular
inspiration

Gain priority access to – and get the chance to shape the focus of – the annual conference for your industry community.

Key
metrics

Access key metrics that aren’t publicly available to benchmark your performance against your peers.

Critical
solutions

Join workflows and sub groups committed to solving your industry’s most pressing shared challenges.
